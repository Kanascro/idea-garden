## Core idea

Users need a simple framework to understand what they are consuming when they use social media.

## Structure

We can imagine a “nutrition label for social media.” Instead of telling people to quit platforms, it teaches them to recognize patterns—attention capture, fragmented focus, emotional amplification, and algorithmic incentives. Awareness allows users to observe these dynamics in their daily use.

## Human signal

The goal is empowerment rather than restriction: understanding the system so people can engage with it consciously.
