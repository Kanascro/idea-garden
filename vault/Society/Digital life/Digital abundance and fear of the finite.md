## Core idea

Growing up with digital platforms can create discomfort with physical scarcity because digital environments feel effectively infinite.

## Structure

Digital tools allow endless copying, undoing, and regeneration without depletion. In contrast, physical materials and real-world experiences are consumable and finite. When creation involves resources that run out or irreversible actions, it introduces a sense of risk that digital environments rarely impose.

## Human signal

The hesitation is not about the activity itself but about the moment something real can be used up.
