## Core idea

The core issue with social media is not the technology itself but the incentives created by advertiser-funded ownership.

## Structure

Platforms optimize for engagement because user attention is sold to advertisers. Maximizing time-on-platform therefore becomes the dominant product goal. This incentive structure encourages addictive design patterns and amplifies content that spreads easily, including outrage, misinformation, and polarization.

## Human signal

The message is not that social media is evil. It is that the system’s economic incentives are misaligned with users’ well-being.
