## Core idea

Feeling disconnected from prescribed success metrics can indicate a mismatch with the system, not personal deficiency.

## Structure

When internal experience conflicts with external expectations, the dominant narrative frames it as inadequacy. Reframing this tension reveals it as feedback: the system’s values do not align with individual rhythms or needs. This opens the possibility of redefining success on different terms.

## Human signal

Relief emerges in recognizing that something isn’t wrong with you—it’s wrong for you.
