## Core idea

Diverse systems can be seen as different attempts to improve human experience.

## Structure

Despite surface-level conflict, many structures emerge from efforts to increase safety, stability, or well-being. Disagreements often arise from differences in perspective, experience, and communication rather than fundamentally opposed intentions. This reframes conflict as misalignment rather than opposition.

## Human signal

Compassion becomes easier when conflict is seen as divergence, not hostility.
