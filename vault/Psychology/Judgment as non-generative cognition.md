## Core idea

Certain forms of judgment inhibit solution-building by closing inquiry.

## Structure

Moral condemnation, outrage, and superiority fix attention on what is wrong without opening pathways for change. By locating problems externally, they reduce introspection and creative engagement. Alternative framing—focused on possibility rather than fault—can sustain momentum toward improvement.

## Human signal

The intention is not to suppress emotion, but to redirect it toward something generative.
