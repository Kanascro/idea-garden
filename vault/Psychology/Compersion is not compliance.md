## Core idea

Feeling joy for someone else’s experiences is not an obligation; it only emerges when safety, trust, and personal alignment are present.

## Structure

Emotional generosity cannot be forced as proof of maturity or openness. If a situation conflicts with one’s values or sense of integrity, the task is not to force acceptance but to recognize the misalignment clearly. Genuine positive feeling toward another’s happiness arises naturally when personal boundaries and ethics remain intact.

## Human signal

Warmth cannot be performed; it appears when the heart feels safe.
