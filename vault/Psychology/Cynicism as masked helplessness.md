## Core idea

Cynical narratives often disguise a deeper sense of powerlessness rather than true indifference.

## Structure

Statements like “nobody cares” or “nothing matters” compress complexity into inevitability, reducing perceived agency. These narratives can serve existing power structures by discouraging participation. What appears as apathy is often overwhelm redirected into resignation.

## Human signal

Beneath the detachment sits a quieter intensity: people care, but don’t see how to act.
