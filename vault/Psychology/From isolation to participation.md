## Core idea

The transition from disengagement to motivation often occurs when individuals move from isolated environments into participatory communities.

## Structure

Isolation amplifies uncertainty about how to begin, what to prioritize, and whether effort matters. Participation provides immediate feedback: others are working, progress is visible, and contribution has recognizable effects. This shift reframes effort from an internal battle to a shared activity with visible outcomes. The presence of others performing meaningful work creates a gravitational pull toward participation.

## Human signal

Engagement tends to appear when people feel part of something that is already moving.
