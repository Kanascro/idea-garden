## Core idea

Under stress, the mind often generates extreme scenarios in an attempt to regain a sense of control.

## Structure

When anxiety is activated, thoughts may rapidly escalate toward drastic solutions such as ending situations, withdrawing completely, or radically changing one’s life. These narratives function as attempts to resolve uncertainty but can feel overwhelming and disproportionate.

## Human signal

In moments of panic, the mind often tries to solve discomfort by imagining total escape.
