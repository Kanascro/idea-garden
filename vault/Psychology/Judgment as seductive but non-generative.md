## Core idea

Moral condemnation provides emotional gratification without generating change.

## Structure

Labeling others as wrong or corrupt simplifies reality and creates a sense of clarity and superiority. However, it halts inquiry and prevents engagement with underlying mechanisms. Systems persist not because they are misunderstood as broken, but because their dynamics are not reimagined.

## Human signal

The sweetness of judgment fades quickly, leaving fatigue instead of movement.
