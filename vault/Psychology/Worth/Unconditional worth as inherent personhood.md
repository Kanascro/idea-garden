## Core idea

Unconditional worth systems detach human value from performance, loyalty, or identity conditions.

## Structure

In this orientation, worth is not conditional on productivity, moral consistency, loyalty, strength, or usefulness. A person’s actions may still be evaluated, guided, or criticized, but those evaluations apply to behavior rather than to the value of the person. This separation allows growth, correction, and accountability without threatening fundamental dignity.

## Human signal

The pressure to constantly prove worth relaxes. You do not need to justify your existence; being here is already sufficient.
