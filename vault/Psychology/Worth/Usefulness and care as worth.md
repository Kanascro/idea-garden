## Core idea

Care-based systems tie worth to being needed, helpful, or indispensable.

## Structure

Recognition emerges through caregiving, service, or emotional labor. These systems are common in caregiving roles and relational environments. Because value comes from supporting others, people can begin to over-function and neglect their own needs.

## Human signal

The danger is quiet self-erasure: the more one helps, the less room remains for oneself.
