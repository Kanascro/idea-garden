## Core idea

Some people resist positive thinking because they fear it may distort their perception of reality.

## Structure

Maintaining a positive mindset can feel risky when individuals worry it might cause them to underestimate the seriousness of their situation. They may fear that optimism creates illusion, leading them to overlook real problems or delay practical solutions.

## Human signal

For some, skepticism toward positivity is a way of protecting themselves from disappointment.
