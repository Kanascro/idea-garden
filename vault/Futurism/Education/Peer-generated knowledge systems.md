## Core idea

Students can become co-creators of educational content rather than passive recipients of predetermined curricula.

## Structure

In collaborative learning systems, participants actively document insights, build shared knowledge resources, and develop projects that contribute to collective understanding. Educational material evolves dynamically as learners add perspectives, refine explanations, and test ideas through practice. This approach recognizes that knowledge grows through participation rather than simple transmission from authority to student.

## Human signal

Education becomes an act of building knowledge together rather than consuming information.
