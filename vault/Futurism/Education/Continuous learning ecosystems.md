## Core idea

Education systems can merge with workplaces so that learning and productive contribution evolve together.

## Structure

Traditional education separates learning from real-world application, with students studying for many years before entering professional environments. A blended model integrates universities and companies into continuous learning ecosystems where students participate in real projects while developing skills. Knowledge production becomes collaborative: learners contribute to research, tools, and learning materials that improve the system for future participants. Education becomes an ongoing, participatory process rather than a finite stage preceding work.

## Human signal

Learning becomes a living process embedded within the fabric of society.
