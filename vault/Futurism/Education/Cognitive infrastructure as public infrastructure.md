## Core idea

A participatory society requires investing in the systems that support understanding, translation, and collective reasoning.

## Structure

Current institutions spend heavily on enforcement, surveillance, persuasion, and status preservation. Those resources could be redirected toward translation, moderation, knowledge auditing, and cross-domain deliberation. The shift is not simply adding more complexity, but reducing epistemic waste: misunderstanding, misalignment, and reactive governance.

## Human signal

The wager is that confusion is already expensive, but its cost is hidden inside the normal functioning of society.
