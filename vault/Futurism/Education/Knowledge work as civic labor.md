## Core idea

Interpretation, synthesis, and epistemic maintenance can be treated as essential civic work rather than intellectual side labor.

## Structure

Tasks like clarifying complex issues, moderating disagreement, tagging knowledge, and building accessible explanations would become socially supported roles. This turns sensemaking into a shared public function rather than an elite byproduct.

## Human signal

Thinking clearly stops being a luxury activity and becomes part of how society cares for itself.
