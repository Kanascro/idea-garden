## Core idea

Language can function as infrastructure when it names dynamics that societies cannot yet clearly see.

## Structure

The project builds a lexicon of distinctions, terms, and practices that help people recognize emerging cultural or relational patterns. Rather than producing commentary or persuasion, the work focuses on creating precise conceptual tools. These tools are meant to circulate freely, be reused without attribution, and remain useful across contexts and generations.

## Human signal

The act resembles tending a garden of meanings—slow cultivation so that others can think more clearly later.
