## Core idea

Political life can be organized through permeable, shifting forms of affiliation rather than rigid territorial separation.

## Structure

Different groups can pursue different priorities, compare outcomes, and evolve through parallel experimentation. This reframes conflict away from competition between nations and toward coexistence between worldviews. Physical space remains shared, but authority becomes more layered and less exclusive.

## Human signal

The fantasy is not unity, but a world where difference no longer requires hard separation.
