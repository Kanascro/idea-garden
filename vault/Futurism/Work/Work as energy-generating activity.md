## Core idea

Work can be designed to replenish energy rather than deplete it.

## Structure

Roles adapt to individuals’ physical, intellectual, and creative needs instead of forcing uniform output. Tasks, rhythms, and environments are shaped to sustain engagement and vitality. Productivity emerges as a byproduct of alignment, not pressure or extraction.

## Human signal

Work stops feeling like something you recover from, and starts feeling like something that feeds you.
