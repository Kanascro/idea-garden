## Core idea

Connection can be structured as a continuously renegotiated agreement rather than a fixed model.

## Structure

Instead of predefined forms, participants regularly revisit what the relationship is, what it offers, and what it needs. Rituals like periodic “state of the connection” conversations replace assumptions of stability. Agreements are living documents—flexible, revisable, and explicit.

## Human signal

The bond feels alive because it is always being consciously shaped.
