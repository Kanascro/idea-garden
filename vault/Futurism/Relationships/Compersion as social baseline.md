## Core idea

A society organized around shared joy would replace comparison and competition with mutual uplift.

## Structure

Compersion—taking pleasure in others’ well-being—would invert many default dynamics. Instead of success creating envy or threat, it would generate collective satisfaction. Social interactions would reinforce expansion rather than scarcity, reducing zero-sum thinking across personal and collective domains.

## Human signal

Joy would feel less private, more contagious—something that spreads instead of divides.
