## Core idea

True pluralism accepts that some systems will persist even if they are seen as flawed or undesirable.

## Structure

By removing intervention, the system allows individuals to choose environments that may not align with external standards of progress. This preserves autonomy but introduces the risk of isolation, stagnation, or self-limitation. The design prioritizes freedom of alignment over universal correction.

## Human signal

The tension is unresolved: freedom is preserved, but not all outcomes feel acceptable.
