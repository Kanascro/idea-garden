## Core idea

A plural system cannot remain plural if it permits projects aimed at erasing plurality itself.

## Structure

The hardest case is a group that seeks control, forced assimilation, extraction, or suppression of dissent. If every value is permitted without limit, domination reappears inside the system. This means coexistence requires at least one meta-constraint: no group may impose itself on others or punish internal departure.

## Human signal

The difficulty is real: protecting freedom without quietly rebuilding the authority that freedom was meant to escape.
