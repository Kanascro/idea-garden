## Core idea

Experts should be placed inside structures that assume capture is always possible.

## Structure

Protection can come from forced plurality: mixed committees, rotating membership, public disclosure of funding and affiliations, conflict-of-interest firewalls, minority reports, and transparent publication of reasoning. No expert body should speak in one seamless voice if real disagreement exists. The system should preserve visible friction so that influence becomes harder to hide and easier to contest.

## Human signal

Trust becomes less about faith in people and more about confidence in the design around them.
