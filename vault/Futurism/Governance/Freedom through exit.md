## Core idea

The right to leave is the primary safeguard against internal constraint.

## Structure

Systems may organize themselves freely, but must maintain real pathways for dissent, voice, and departure. Mobility—both cognitive and physical—acts as a continuous accountability mechanism. If participation is voluntary and reversible, coercion becomes structurally harder to sustain.

## Human signal

Freedom is anchored not in agreement, but in the ability to walk away.
