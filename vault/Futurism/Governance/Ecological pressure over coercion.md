## Core idea

Systems survive or decline based on their ability to sustain participation and coherence.

## Structure

Without enforcement, growth depends on attracting and retaining people. Systems that adapt, resonate, and remain open tend to expand, while rigid or harmful ones may stagnate or fragment. This creates a landscape where viability is tested through interaction rather than control.

## Human signal

There is no guarantee of justice—only the ongoing movement of alignment and withdrawal.
