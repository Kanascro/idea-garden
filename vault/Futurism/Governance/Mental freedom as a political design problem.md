## Core idea

A democratic system must protect citizens from lobbying, propaganda, and other forces that capture thought before choice is even made.

## Structure

If opinion formation is manipulated upstream, voting cannot be fully free downstream. The question is not only how people vote, but how they arrive at beliefs at all. This includes suspicion toward obvious persuasion systems and also toward normalized institutions like school, which may shape thought under the guise of education.

## Human signal

The concern is not just corruption of decisions, but corruption of consciousness itself.
