## Core idea

Holistic committees should be built to create productive clash between kinds of knowledge, not false consensus.

## Structure

A committee could include disciplinary experts, hybrid cross-domain thinkers, affected citizens, and dedicated translators of complexity. Their task would not be to erase disagreement quickly, but to expose where models conflict, where assumptions differ, and where trade-offs become unavoidable. This makes committees less like temples of expertise and more like structured engines of comparison.

## Human signal

The image is not harmony first, but intelligent friction that produces a more complete picture.
