## Core idea

Collective decision-making can separate what people want from what can be physically executed.

## Structure

Values and goals can be formed independent of location, while implementation must align with material constraints like land, infrastructure, and ecosystems. Individuals participate in both layers: one shaping desired directions, the other negotiating what is feasible locally. This prevents symbolic alignment from drifting too far from real-world consequences.

## Human signal

The tension resolves by accepting that imagination travels freely, but reality does not.
