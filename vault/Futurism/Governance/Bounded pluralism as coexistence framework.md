## Core idea

A plural system can allow radically different ways of living while maintaining minimal conditions for coexistence.

## Structure

Groups operate autonomously without imposing their worldview externally. A thin shared layer defines baseline conditions—no coercion, protection of autonomy, and the right to leave—that make coexistence possible. This separates moral disagreement from structural incompatibility, allowing diversity without collapse into conflict.

## Human signal

The system accepts deep difference without needing to resolve it into agreement.
