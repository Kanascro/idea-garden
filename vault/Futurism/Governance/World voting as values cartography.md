## Core idea

A civic app could map what people actually value by asking them to vote on specific matters directly rather than through party identity.

## Structure

The system would treat politics as a live cartography of public priorities, not a competition between packaged ideologies. Citizens would first reveal value alignments and desired goals issue by issue. Only after that would political discussion move toward the practical question of means.

## Human signal

The impulse here is to see people more clearly than parties do and to build politics around what minds actually contain.
