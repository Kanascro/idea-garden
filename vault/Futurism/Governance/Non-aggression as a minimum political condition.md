## Core idea

Coexistence depends on a baseline rule that internal autonomy does not include the right to dominate externally.

## Structure

Groups may govern themselves differently, but actions affecting others must be constrained by shared anti-aggression norms. Violations can trigger loss of trust, collaboration, and participation privileges rather than immediate coercive takeover. This preserves difference while preventing pluralism from becoming cover for expansionism.

## Human signal

The system draws one hard line so that many softer differences can survive.
