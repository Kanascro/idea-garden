## Core idea

Information systems must actively detect and expose influence rather than assume neutrality.

## Structure

Mechanisms like provenance tracing, multi-axis reputation, narrative audits, and protected contrarian spaces create visibility into how ideas form and spread. Instead of suppressing fringe views, the system contains and evaluates them, allowing valid insights to surface while tracking manipulation patterns.

## Human signal

The aim is not purity of information, but resilience of minds navigating it.
