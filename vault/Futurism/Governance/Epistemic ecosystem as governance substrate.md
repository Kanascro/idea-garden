## Core idea

Knowledge production becomes a civic infrastructure designed for pluralism, not a pipeline toward singular truth.

## Structure

The system treats knowledge as evolving, situated, and contestable, replacing static authority with dynamic interpretation. Multiple expert panels generate parallel models rather than unified conclusions, allowing citizens to navigate competing framings. Decision-making shifts from enforcing correctness to enabling informed exploration across perspectives.

## Human signal

The orientation moves from “Who is right?” to “What lenses are available to think with?”
