## Core idea

A small set of shared constraints enables pluralism without enforcing uniform values.

## Structure

These commitments do not define what is true or good, but what must be preserved for systems to coexist: no forced belief, protection of bodies, access to learning, and prevention of domination. They function as boundary conditions, not ideological content.

## Human signal

The system draws a thin line—not to define truth, but to prevent harm that collapses choice.
