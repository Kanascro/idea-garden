## Core idea

Expertise can be positioned as a tool for exploring implementation rather than defining goals.

## Structure

Once goals are identified collectively, domain specialists model possible pathways, trade-offs, and consequences. Their role is not to decide what should be done, but to clarify what can be done and at what cost. This reframes expertise as interpretive and exploratory rather than authoritative.

## Human signal

There is respect for knowledge, but resistance to it becoming power.
