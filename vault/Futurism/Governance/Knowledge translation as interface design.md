## Core idea

Understanding requires dedicated roles that translate between cognitive worlds without flattening them.

## Structure

Knowledge translators function as epistemic designers, shaping how ideas are accessed and compared. They expose assumptions, align vocabularies, and create multiple entry points—visual, narrative, technical—so users can engage at different depths. Translation happens during knowledge creation, not after, preserving nuance while expanding accessibility.

## Human signal

Complexity is not reduced; it is made navigable.
