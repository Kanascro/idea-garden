## Core idea

Expert panels are designed to surface and work through disagreement rather than eliminate it.

## Structure

Panels include diverse epistemic roles—specialists, systems thinkers, cultural lenses, contrarians—and operate through explicit tension. Outputs document conflicting models, unresolved questions, and trade-offs instead of forcing consensus. Structured friction becomes the mechanism through which more complete understanding emerges.

## Human signal

Disagreement is reframed from failure into a productive force.
