## Core idea

The goal is not perfect representation of people, but better conditions for self-reflection and alignment.

## Structure

Accuracy is treated as instrumental, not absolute—useful insofar as it helps individuals and groups orient, connect, and evolve. The system supports exploration of one’s own patterns rather than claiming definitive knowledge about them. This reframes mapping as a tool for awareness, not surveillance.

## Human signal

Clarity is offered as an invitation, not imposed as a verdict.
