## Core idea

Expertise is tracked as a multidimensional, evolving reputation rather than a fixed title.

## Structure

Credentials emerge from peer communities and reflect specific capacities—rigor, clarity, integration, predictive accuracy—rather than general status. They are revocable, contextual, and cumulative across contributions. This allows expertise to be both legible and fluid, supporting cross-disciplinary recognition without collapsing into hierarchy.

## Human signal

Trust becomes granular and earned over time, not granted once and protected indefinitely.
