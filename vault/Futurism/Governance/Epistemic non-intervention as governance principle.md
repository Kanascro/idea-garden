## Core idea

A system can refuse to override value systems, allowing them to evolve through interaction rather than imposed correction.

## Structure

No external authority enforces moral hierarchy, even when some systems appear regressive or harmful. Instead of intervention, the design enables visibility, comparison, and movement between systems. Change emerges through exposure, imitation, and voluntary departure rather than top-down reform.

## Human signal

This stance trades the comfort of intervention for the discomfort of letting others remain different.
