## Core idea

All knowledge carries perspective, and making that visible enhances rather than weakens understanding.

## Structure

Historical and contemporary ideas are annotated with contextual tags—cultural, temporal, ideological—allowing users to interpret them through multiple lenses. Knowledge becomes a layered graph with ancestry and reinterpretation, where bias is navigated rather than erased.

## Human signal

Truth is no longer treated as neutral ground, but as terrain shaped by many hands.
