## Core idea

A system is only genuinely voluntary if people can dissent, leave, and reaffiliate without retaliation.

## Structure

Any claim to self-governance becomes suspect when members are trapped by force, dependency, or punishment. Mobility functions as both a personal right and a structural safeguard against ideological capture. Without real exit, internal freedom becomes rhetoric rather than design.

## Human signal

Freedom is measured less by how a system describes itself than by what happens when someone tries to walk away.
