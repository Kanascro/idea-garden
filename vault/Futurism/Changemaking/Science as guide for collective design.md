## Core idea

Empirical understanding of human well-being can inform how systems are built and adjusted.

## Structure

Research from psychology, neuroscience, sociology, health sciences, anthropology, Eastern and traditional knowledge provides insight into what conditions support thriving. Policies and structures can be treated as experiments, continuously refined based on observed outcomes. This introduces adaptability and learning into governance.

## Human signal

There is trust in learning together rather than claiming to already know.
