## Core idea

Growth is replaced by a bundle of measures: mission success, well-being outcomes, ecological integrity, and capability expansion.

## Structure

Mission success defines direction through concrete outcomes like clean energy or resilient cities. Well-being metrics track lived conditions such as housing and mental health. Ecological indicators prevent success from being defined through damage. Capability measures track skills, research capacity, and shared infrastructure, ensuring future problem-solving ability.

## Human signal

Ambition remains, but its meaning changes. The goal is not more output, but more life-supporting capacity.
