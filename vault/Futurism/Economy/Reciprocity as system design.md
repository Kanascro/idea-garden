## Core idea

Economic and relational systems can be built on voluntary reciprocity instead of enforced exchange.

## Structure

Participants are given multiple ways to return value—financial, narrative, or relational. This diversifies contribution and reduces dependence on a single transactional path. The system adapts to different capacities while maintaining coherence through shared values.

## Human signal

A sense of participation in something living—where giving back feels natural, not required.
