## Core idea

Biological intelligence remains functional because it forgets, while digital systems overwhelm because they attempt to remember everything.

## Structure

Human memory naturally prunes, fades, and reconstructs information over time. This forgetting process prevents overload and allows meaningful patterns to emerge. Digital systems, by contrast, treat storage as permanent accumulation, producing environments where nothing decays and attention becomes saturated.

## Human signal

The mind breathes through cycles of remembering and forgetting.
