## Core idea

Digital tools can act as light scaffolding that supports human cognition rather than replacing it.

## Structure

In this approach, technology introduces information slowly, detects cognitive saturation, and encourages consolidation before further input. Stored material resurfaces periodically and fades unless consciously renewed. The system expands and contracts with a person’s attention rather than demanding constant engagement.

## Human signal

The tool behaves like a quiet companion to the mind.
