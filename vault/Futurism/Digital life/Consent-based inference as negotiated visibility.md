## Core idea

Data use can be structured as an ongoing negotiation rather than a one-time permission.

## Structure

Individuals choose which domains are visible, review inferred patterns, and actively confirm or reject interpretations. This creates a feedback loop where the system proposes meaning, but the person retains authorship over their own profile. Visibility becomes modular, adjustable, and revisable over time.

## Human signal

Being seen becomes collaborative, not extractive.
