## Core idea

A humane social platform can be designed as civic infrastructure for meeting new people rather than as a marketplace optimizing attraction or engagement.

## Structure

Unlike typical platforms, the system does not frame interaction as searching for partners, maximizing visibility, or competing for attention. It functions as a social commons where people encounter one another gradually within a shared environment designed for trust and safety. The goal is not romantic success but the cultivation of human connection.

## Human signal

The central question shifts from “Who do I want?” to “How do I want to belong?”
