## Core idea

A humane social platform requires structural safeguards that prevent it from becoming an attention marketplace.

## Structure

The system must reject profit-driven incentives, advertising, engagement optimization, desirability rankings, follower metrics, and endless feeds. Romantic or sexual outcomes cannot be assumed as the default purpose of participation. Vulnerability and exposure must also be balanced so no group carries disproportionate social risk.

## Human signal

The design refuses to treat people as products competing for attention.
