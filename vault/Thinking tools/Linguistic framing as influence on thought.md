## Core idea

Control or limitation of language can shape how people think and what they consider possible.

## Structure

When vocabulary is restricted or framed narrowly, certain ideas become harder to conceive or challenge. Conversely, exposure to multiple linguistic systems expands cognitive flexibility by offering alternative structures of thought. Language is not neutral—it guides attention and interpretation.

## Human signal

There is a subtle caution: what you can’t say, you may not fully see.
