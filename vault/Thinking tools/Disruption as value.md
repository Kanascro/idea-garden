## Core idea

The function of insight is not to resolve tension but to reveal and reframe it.

## Structure

Rather than providing clean solutions, ideas are used to unsettle assumptions and expose hidden structures. This creates second-order effects—changes in perspective that may alter decisions indirectly. Value lies in transformation of viewpoint, not immediate answers.

## Human signal

A subtle unease paired with curiosity—the sense that something has shifted, even if it’s not yet clear how.
