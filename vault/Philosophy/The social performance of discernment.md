## Core idea

Discernment is often used to signal identity rather than to deepen understanding.

## Structure

Concepts like taste, standards, or intelligence can function as coded judgments that establish separation. These signals reinforce self-image by contrasting against others, creating a refined but limiting identity structure. The result is a constrained worldview that appears elevated while remaining closed.

## Human signal

What presents as growth may instead be a subtle form of self-protection.
