## Core idea

Expression can share perspective without asserting authority over others.

## Structure

Statements grounded in personal experience leave room for disagreement, while those framed as universal claims implicitly demand alignment. The distinction is not in content but in posture: whether language invites plurality or closes it. This creates a spectrum between sharing and imposing, even when intentions are similar.

## Human signal

There is care here: a desire to speak without constraining others.
