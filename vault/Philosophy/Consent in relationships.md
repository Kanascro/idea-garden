## Core idea

Safe relationships integrate consent into every dimension of interaction, from physical contact to emotional and intellectual exchange.

## Structure

Consent-based relationships involve continuous awareness of mutual comfort and willingness. Partners ask rather than assume, listen rather than override, and remain attentive to changing boundaries over time. This dynamic extends beyond explicit requests; it includes sensitivity to hesitation, uncertainty, or withdrawal. By prioritizing voluntary participation, relationships maintain trust and psychological safety.

## Human signal

Connection deepens when both people feel free to say “yes,” “no,” or “not now.”
