## Core idea

Expression and behavior operate under different logics and should not be conflated.

## Structure

Language can remain non-imposing while actions inevitably shape shared reality. A belief expressed as personal may still carry force when enacted. This introduces a second layer of responsibility: how values are lived, not just how they are stated. The boundary between sharing and imposing becomes more complex at the level of action.

## Human signal

There is an emerging awareness that living a value carries weight beyond words.
