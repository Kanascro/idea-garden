## Core idea

Entropy in society appears as growing complexity, fragmentation, and loss of coherence across systems.

## Structure

As systems scale—political, economic, informational—they accumulate layers, exceptions, and interactions that become harder to track or manage. This creates opacity, where cause and effect are no longer clear. Individuals face systems that feel unpredictable and uncontrollable, not because they are broken, but because they have become too complex to intuitively grasp.

## Human signal

There is a sense of overload: too many moving parts to hold together.
