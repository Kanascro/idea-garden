## Core idea

Complex systems drift away from their original purposes as incentives compound.

## Structure

Organizations optimize for measurable outputs (profit, growth, engagement), which can diverge from human well-being over time. Feedback loops reinforce these metrics, even when they produce unintended consequences. The system continues to function, but no longer aligns with its initial intent.

## Human signal

There is a subtle unease: things are working, but not in the way they should.
