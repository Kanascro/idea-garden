## Core idea

Extending unconditional respect to all beings and things reframes interaction from control to reverence.

## Structure

This approach removes hierarchical valuation—human over animal, subject over object—and replaces it with consistent dignity across contexts. Communication shifts toward nonviolent expression, where needs are acknowledged without blame or domination. Behavior becomes guided by care rather than judgment, dissolving many sources of conflict at their root.

## Human signal

There is a softness here that borders on sacred: everything is met as worthy of care.
