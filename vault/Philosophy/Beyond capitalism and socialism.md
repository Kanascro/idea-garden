## Core idea

Economic systems can be reframed as different strategies toward the same underlying goal: human well-being.

## Structure

Rather than treating ideologies as opposing truths, they can be understood as partial approaches shaped by different assumptions about how to achieve flourishing. By recognizing shared intent, conflict shifts from opposition to comparison of methods. This opens the possibility of synthesis rather than competition between frameworks.

## Human signal

Less need to defend, more space to integrate.
