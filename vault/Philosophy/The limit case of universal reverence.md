## Core idea

Applying equal respect to all entities introduces tensions between values, needs, and practical action.

## Structure

Treating everything with reverence raises questions of trade-offs: survival, resource use, and conflicting needs cannot be avoided. The system must therefore handle situations where respect does not lead to harmony but to negotiation between incompatible realities. Reverence becomes a guiding posture, not a guarantee of resolution.

## Human signal

The ideal remains intact, but reality presses against it.
