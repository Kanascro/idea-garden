## Core idea

“Right” can be reframed as what is genuinely present, not what is correct or desirable.

## Structure

Inner signals are not evaluated for accuracy or health but acknowledged as current reality. This removes the need to categorize experiences as valid or distorted, allowing them to be engaged without resistance. The focus shifts from fixing or correcting to noticing and responding, reducing internal conflict.

## Human signal

There is a radical softness here: nothing inside needs to justify its existence.
