## Core idea

Human experience can organize itself without imposed control when allowed to unfold.

## Structure

Even when shaped by conditioning or suppression, internal signals are assumed to recalibrate through non-judgmental engagement. Basic needs must still be met, but beyond that, coherence emerges from responsiveness rather than correction. This reflects both a philosophical stance and lived experience.

## Human signal

There is a deep trust here, born not from theory alone but from something personally rediscovered.
