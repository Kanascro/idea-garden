## Core idea

Broadening perception can be treated as the highest-leverage use of attention.

## Structure

Expanding one’s view—through new perspectives, disciplines, or experiences—increases the range of what can be understood and acted upon. This compounds over time, as wider context reshapes interpretation of all incoming information. Rather than optimizing within a narrow frame, the frame itself evolves.

## Human signal

There is a sense of opening: the world keeps getting bigger.
