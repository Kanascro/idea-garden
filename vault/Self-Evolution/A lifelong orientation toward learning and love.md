## Core idea

Some lives are organized around a long-term trajectory of curiosity, relational openness, and sustained engagement with the world.

## Structure

This orientation values continuous learning, expanding understanding, and maintaining emotional connection to people and ideas throughout life. Rather than narrowing with age, the goal is to remain receptive to discovery and relational depth. Protecting this trajectory requires deliberate choices about environments, relationships, and commitments that either sustain fascination or erode it. Cynicism and numbness signal conditions that undermine the long arc of growth.

## Human signal

The guiding question becomes whether a path keeps the mind awake and the heart connected.
