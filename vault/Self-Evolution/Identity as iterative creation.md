## Core idea

The self is not fixed but continuously re-authored through transitions.

## Structure

Each rupture interrupts continuity and forces a reassessment of who one is. New identities are not discovered fully formed but built from available fragments—experiences, desires, and meaning-making. This process repeats across life, making identity an evolving construct rather than a stable core.

## Human signal

There is beauty in realizing you are something you keep creating.
