## Core idea

Failure can be understood as a mismatch between intention and outcome rather than a definitive negative.

## Structure

Outcomes diverge from expectations due to incomplete information, uncertainty, or changing conditions. Labeling this divergence as failure adds a layer of meaning that is not inherent to the event. Removing the label allows the situation to be seen as information rather than judgment.

## Human signal

There is lightness in seeing things as feedback instead of verdicts.
