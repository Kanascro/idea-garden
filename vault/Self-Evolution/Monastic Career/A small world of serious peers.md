## Core idea

Intellectual companionship is necessary, but it must exist without the pressures of performance or leadership.

## Structure

To avoid isolation, the plan includes periodic embedding in environments where thinking is shared—research labs, institutes, libraries, or cultural houses. These spaces are not audiences but peer worlds: places where conversation and inquiry can unfold naturally without the demand to manage, persuade, or inspire others.

## Human signal

The desire is for colleagues to think with, not followers to guide.
