## Core idea

Financial support should come from institutions rather than extracting income from individuals.

## Structure

Sustainability comes through fellowships, residencies, stipends, and civic or cultural research funding. This model protects the work from dependence on persuasion, urgency, or personal branding. Money becomes a background condition that enables the practice rather than defining its value.

## Human signal

The aim is freedom from hustling—space to think without turning attention into a commodity.
