## Core idea

Personal history can be reinterpreted through compassion rather than judgment.

## Structure

Experiences from the past need not remain colored by regret or shame. By revisiting them with softness and acceptance, the narrative of one’s life can be reframed without denying what happened. The goal is not distortion but kindness toward earlier versions of oneself.

## Human signal

Memory softens when it is held with warmth instead of criticism.
