## Core idea

Reducing reliance on judgment can lead to a more stable and less conflicted inner experience.

## Structure

Judgment introduces constant evaluation, comparison, and correction, which increases mental friction. Letting go of fixed categories reduces this tension, enabling a calmer and more continuous flow of thought. This is not positioned as universally necessary, but as conducive to well-being.

## Human signal

The aim is not correctness, but a quieter mind.
