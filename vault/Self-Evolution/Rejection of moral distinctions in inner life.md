## Core idea

Distinctions like validation versus indulgence are forms of imposed judgment that disrupt internal coherence.

## Structure

Labeling responses as excessive or appropriate introduces external standards into inner experience. This creates fragmentation by dividing impulses into acceptable and unacceptable categories. Removing these distinctions allows a more continuous relationship with oneself, where responses are not pre-filtered.

## Human signal

There is a refusal to split oneself into parts that must be managed.
