## Core idea

Imagining a life built entirely around what fulfills a person clarifies the values that genuinely matter.

## Structure

When fear-based compromise is removed from the equation, priorities often shift toward experiences that generate vitality, growth, exploration, and embodied wellbeing. This exercise reveals the activities, environments, and rhythms that naturally sustain a sense of aliveness.

## Human signal

The question itself opens space to imagine a life that feels expansive rather than constrained.
