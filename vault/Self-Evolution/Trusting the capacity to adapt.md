## Core idea

The ability to adjust after a decision matters more than predicting the perfect outcome beforehand.

## Structure

Life rarely unfolds exactly as expected, and decisions lead to new information rather than final answers. Trusting one’s capacity to learn, recalibrate, and respond reduces the fear of choosing incorrectly.

## Human signal

Confidence grows from knowing that paths can be changed. Building resilience is more important than doing the "right" choices.
