## Core idea

Removing evaluation from inner experience restores sensitivity and trust in oneself.

## Structure

Categorizing thoughts and impulses as good or bad disrupts responsiveness and creates internal friction. By suspending judgment, all signals become usable, allowing perception to recalibrate naturally. This shifts the system from suppression to responsiveness, enabling clearer orientation over time.

## Human signal

There is a softness here: nothing inside needs to be corrected to be followed.
