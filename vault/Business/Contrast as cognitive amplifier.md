## Core idea

Perceived value increases when an option is compared with a familiar alternative that carries higher cost or difficulty.

## Structure

Contrast helps people quickly evaluate usefulness by placing an offer within a recognizable frame of reference. When a tool or service is positioned relative to a known expense or effort—such as therapy, training programs, or extensive research—the difference becomes easier to grasp. The comparison highlights efficiency and accessibility without requiring complex explanation. This cognitive shortcut helps individuals understand the benefit instantly.

## Human signal

A simple comparison can make a value proposition immediately understandable and compelling.
