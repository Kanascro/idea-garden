## Core idea

Boredom signals friction between different internal modes forced into a single framework.

## Structure

One mode seeks order, clarity, and completion, while another pushes toward exploration, invention, and expansion. When both are compressed into a productivity system, neither operates well. This creates paralysis disguised as boredom. Recognizing multiple modes removes the pressure to resolve them into one.

## Human signal

The tension becomes less personal and more structural. There is relief in seeing multiplicity instead of failure.
