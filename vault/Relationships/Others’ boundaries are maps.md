## Core idea

When someone states a boundary, they are describing their limits rather than imposing a cage.

## Structure

A boundary communicates where a person stands and what conditions they need. Hearing it does not create obligation; it provides information that allows the other person to decide whether they can meet that condition. If the boundary does not work for both people, the mismatch becomes visible rather than hidden.

## Human signal

Boundaries reveal the terrain of a relationship so each person can choose their path.
