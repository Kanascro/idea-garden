## Core idea

Certain forms of recognition activate vitality in a person.

## Structure

Being perceived through a lens that matches one’s inner intensity can amplify presence, creativity, and emotional energy. When someone sees and names those qualities—intense, electric, mythic—it creates resonance between inner experience and external recognition. The question invites the other person to engage with that perception consciously rather than passively.

## Human signal

Coming alive depends not only on being loved but on being _seen in the right way_. The invitation carries curiosity and vulnerability: an opening for someone to look more deeply.
