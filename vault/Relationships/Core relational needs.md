## Core idea

Relationships often revolve around a set of recurring human needs.

## Structure

Common needs include meaningful connection, vitality through stimulation and creativity, autonomy to follow personal rhythms, recognition of one’s authentic self, integrity without suppressing preferences, and co-creation where both partners influence shared life. When these needs remain unspoken, misalignment grows.

## Human signal

The longing is not only for partnership but for aliveness within it.
