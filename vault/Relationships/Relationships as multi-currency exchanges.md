## Core idea

All relationships operate through exchanges of intangible resources like attention, care, and trust.

## Structure

Instead of money, relational systems run on flows of presence, emotional support, reliability, and shared experiences. Different relationships emphasize different “currencies,” but the underlying dynamic remains: value is created, exchanged, and perceived. This makes relationships structurally similar despite differing forms or labels.

## Human signal

Connection starts to feel like something alive and circulating, not fixed.
