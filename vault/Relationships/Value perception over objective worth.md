## Core idea

Relational “currencies” have no fixed value; they depend on how they are received.

## Structure

The same action—time, touch, words—can carry different weight depending on the person and context. Value is co-created through perception rather than inherent in the act itself. This makes relational exchange fluid, subjective, and continuously renegotiated.

## Human signal

What matters is not what is given, but how it lands.
