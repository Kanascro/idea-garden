## Core idea

A partner’s unresolved fear can interfere with their ability to offer stable commitment.

## Structure

Fear of loss, change, or responsibility may cause someone to hesitate, withdraw, or remain ambiguous about long-term intentions. When these fears persist without being addressed, the other partner may experience chronic uncertainty. This creates a dynamic where one person seeks security while the other remains guarded.

## Human signal

The relationship can start to feel like waiting for reassurance that never fully arrives.
