## Core idea

A sustainable relationship requires a partner who recognizes that diminishing oneself for love is not virtue but damage.

## Structure

Some relational norms frame sacrifice and self-erasure as proof of devotion. This reframing rejects that model: when a person must shrink, mute, or erase parts of themselves to maintain the bond, the relationship structure is already harmful. A compatible partner must see self-preservation and self-expression as necessary conditions for love, not threats to it.

## Human signal

The desire is not merely to be loved, but to be protected from disappearing inside that love. Recognition becomes a safeguard for one’s aliveness and integrity.
