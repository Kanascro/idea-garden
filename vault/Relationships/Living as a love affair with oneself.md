## Core idea

A life organized around self-affection, curiosity, and creativity can become a sustaining foundation independent of any single relationship.

## Structure

When individuals cultivate routines that celebrate their own existence—caring for their bodies, exploring interests, expressing creativity, and nurturing emotional honesty—they build a stable source of meaning that does not rely on external validation. Relationships then become additions to an already vibrant life rather than the sole container for fulfillment. This orientation transforms personal growth from recovery into ongoing exploration.

## Human signal

The central relationship in life becomes the one a person maintains with their own aliveness.
