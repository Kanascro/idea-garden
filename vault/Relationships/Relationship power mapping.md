## Core idea

Mapping relational dynamics can reveal how needs, rhythms, and decision structures distribute power within a relationship.

## Structure

The relationship involves examining how behaviors, needs, and structural decisions shape influence and adaptation. Observing patterns of energy, decision authority, and emotional labor helps identify where one partner consistently adjusts to maintain stability. This map focuses on needs, relational models, and implicit power dynamics rather than blame.

## Human signal

Clarity does not erase care. It simply makes the invisible structure of the relationship easier to see.
