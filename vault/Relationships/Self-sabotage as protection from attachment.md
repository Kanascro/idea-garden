## Core idea

Feelings of unworthiness can lead people to destabilize relationships before deeper attachment forms.

## Structure

Breaking agreements, pushing limits, or creating conflict can create distance that prevents vulnerability. These behaviors may not arise from lack of care but from fear that closeness will eventually lead to rejection or loss. Disrupting stability becomes a way to control the risk of being hurt.

## Human signal

Sometimes the impulse to push someone away appears exactly where caring is strongest.
