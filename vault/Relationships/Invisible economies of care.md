## Core idea

What sustains relationships is often unspoken, yet follows recognizable patterns of contribution and balance.

## Structure

Acts like listening, showing up consistently, or offering affection function as deposits into relational stability. Imbalances—overgiving or under-participation—can destabilize connection even if never explicitly tracked. These economies operate implicitly, shaping how relationships grow or dissolve.

## Human signal

There is a quiet awareness that effort is always being felt, even when it’s not named.
